How it works?

Create and publish your ads using the MarksUp website and get help with your studies from students like you!
Commissions

The transfer of funds to the Contractor's account is carried out at his request. For the withdrawal of funds from the Contractor's Balance, a commission of 10 (ten) percent is charged, deducted from the amount withdrawn by the Contractor.

When the Customer pays for the transaction, a commission of 10 (ten) percent is charged, in addition to the order amount.
